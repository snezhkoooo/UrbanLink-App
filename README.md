# UrbanLink-App
About:
UrbanLink is a modern mobile application that combines real-time urban transportation services with integrated social media features. The platform allows users not only to book rides quickly and safely, but also to share experiences, photos, and interact with other users directly inside the app.

Key Features:
	•	Ride Booking & Tracking
Request rides safely, track ratings and manage trip history.
	•	Social Feed
Post images, short videos, statuses, and interact with other users just like on a any social platform.
	•	User Profiles
Personal profile pages with ride statistics, posts and activity logs.
	•	Driver Profiles
View driver ratings, past trips, reviews, and shared posts.
	•	Integrated Messaging
In-app chat for communication between passengers and drivers.
	•	Safety Tools
All drivers must be verified through our Guidelines and Terms of Service.
	•	Location-Based Discovery
Explore popular places, recommended routes, and trending posts in your city.

Project Goal:
UrbanLink aims to unify urban mobility and social interaction into one seamless ecosystem, making transportation not only more efficient but also more engaging and community-driven.
